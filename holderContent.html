Hello

My name is Matt and I wear a lot of hats. I am a front end developer and interactive designer. I also take photographs and like to write about the state of digital communication, emerging trends on the web and bike racing. I have had a long and circuitous path leading me to my current role, but every step has informed where I am today. I believe strongly in the egalitarian role of information and I hope to facilitate its deployment to everyone, regardless of media or abilities. This site is a sample of my work and a portal to communicate. You can email me and view my resume if you'd like.
